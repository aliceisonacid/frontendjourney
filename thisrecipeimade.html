Reeses Peanut Butter Banana Bread

As most women know, PMS will have our tastebuds completely out of whack!
But with madness comes innovation & so I present, the Reeses peanut butter banana bread
Now I have never been a fan of PB for reasons I wont get into but as of late, i've been
craving it. Completely obsessed, PB&J sandwiches, cracker sandwiches,
PB & Banana sammiches-everything.
Anyway without further adeu, here is my special recipe:

Ingredients:

  1. Almond Flour/All Purpose
  2. Baking Soda
  3. Baking Powder
  4. Sea Salt (Rock)
  5. Overly Ripe Bananas
  6. Smooth Peanut Butter
  7. Ghee/Vegetable Oil
  8. Eggs
  9. Agave Syrup/Sugar
  10. King Size Reeses Cups

    11. The Particulars

    • Prep Time: 15-25min.
    • Cook Time: 60-90 min
    • Total Time: 1hr 30 min.
    • Serving size: 5
    • Method: Oven

    sidebar♥

    You can utilize parchment paper inside of your loaf pan but
    if that isnt available, then be sure to coat it very well.
    The recipe has a good amount of agave/sugar added and will stick
    as the bread rises.
    Also Please dont vigorously mix the batter if it has noticable lumps!
    Doing this will mess with the consistency and texture of the bread,
    turning into a crumble cake unintentionally.

    • 1½ cups of all purpose flour
    • ½ tsp of baking soda
    • 1 ½ tsp of baking powder
    • ½ tsp sea salt
    • 3 extremely ripe bananas-mashed
    • ½ cup of peanut butter
    • ¼ cup of Melted Ghee
    • 2 eggs
    • 1 cup of Agave Syrup
    • 6 King Size Reeses Pieces Cut up


    Instructions

    1. Preheat your oven to 350° F/ 177° C. Please use this time to
      line your loaf pan with either parchment paper and ghee.
    2. In a large bowl, mix your flour,baking soda,baking powder
      & Salt together to a smooth consistency
    3. In a separate bowl, mix the mashed bananas,
      peanut butter,melted ghee,eggs and Agave together
    4. Combine the mixes and stir until everything is evenly coated.
      Remember your batter will be lumpy but this is okay
    5. Slowly Stir in your reeses pieces then pour batter
      into the prepped pan
    6. Bake for 55-65 minutes or until a toothpick/fork
      thats inserted comes out clean
    7. Allow the bread to cool for roughly 15 min.
      then transfer to a fancy plate to cool completely
